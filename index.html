If you are looking for a thriving career that grows with you and challenges your creativity, let Rend Lake College get you started by enrolling in our Graphic Design program and /or Graphic Design Certificate options! Graphic designers decide the most effective way of getting a message across in print, electronic, and film media using a variety of methods, such as color, type, illustration, photography, animation and various print and layout techniques.They help to motivate, inspire, inform, and communicate ideas, concepts and brands by visually problem solving for a particular audience.Graphic designers use a variety of visual solutions and computer software, such as Adobe applications, to develop and create their design solutions.

Graphic designers are instrumental in the creation of:
Layout & design of magazines and other publications
Promotional displays
Product packaging
Advertising and marketing materials
Logos and signage
Web sites, video, mobile applications and other multimedia
